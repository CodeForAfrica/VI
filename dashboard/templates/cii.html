Contextual Vulnerability Index (CVI)

Combines structural vulnerability (debt, military presence, resource dependency) with narrative activity (media volume and tone).

🔹 Structural Vulnerability

Based on debt levels, military ties, resource contracts, and geopolitical exposure.

🔹 Media Volume

Number of articles mentioning the actor in the country (normalized).

🔹 Tone Score

Ranges from -1.0 (alarmist/negative) to +1.0 (positive/supportive).

CVI Formula

CVI = Structural_Vulnerability × min(Volume/100, 1) × (1 - Tone/2)

Result: 0 (no vulnerability) to 1 (maximum vulnerability)
