Contextual Vulnerability Index (CVI)

The CVI quantifies vulnerability by combining structural exposure with real-time media influence.

Structural Vulnerability

Debt levels, military ties, and resource contracts.

Media Volume

Frequency of actor mentions in local media.

Tone Score

Sentiment analysis ranging from -1.0 to +1.0.

Mathematical Formula

CVI = SV × min(V/100, 1) × (1 - T/2)
